About Us


37 Years of Remya Group has set new benchmarks in the restaurant industry.The journey began in 1986 under the visionary leadership of Krishnan.V.P,Gireesh Babu and Rajeesh P.P ,who opened the first Remya Hotel in Vatakara,Calicut,Kerala.
Remya Hotel continues to set the highest standard of taste and services.We hope you have a Remya experience filled with unmatched taste, delightful contentment and memories to cherish.
